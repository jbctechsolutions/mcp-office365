## Support Policy

**This is a part-time, hobby project maintained by JBC Tech Solutions.**

### Response Times (Best Effort)
- 🐛 **Bug reports:** 1-2 weeks
- ✨ **Feature requests:** Reviewed periodically
- 🔀 **Pull requests:** 2-4 weeks
- 🔒 **Security issues:** Prioritized, ~1 week

### What This Means
- Responses are not guaranteed within specific timeframes
- Maintenance happens during maintainer's available time
- Community contributions are encouraged and appreciated
- Critical security issues receive priority

## Reporting Security Issues

**DO NOT** open public issues for security vulnerabilities.

**Email:** support@jbc.dev with:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

We'll respond within 1 week and work with you on a fix.
